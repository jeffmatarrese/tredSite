About our firm

Our story

Tred Architecture + Design is a collaboration between husband-wife design team Megan Tremain and Lee Edwards. After meeting in architecture school, they moved out to the Pacific Northwest to start a new life together. While Tred officially launched in 2014, Megan and Lee have been designing custom homes together for well over a decade, starting with their own. Tred is uniquely positioned to help our clients because we have been in your shoes. We understand your concerns, hopes, and fears, and can guide you through our experience on both sides of the table.
