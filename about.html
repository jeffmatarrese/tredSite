About our firm

Our story

Tred Architecture + Design is a collaboration between husband-wife design team Megan Tremain and Lee Edwards. After meeting in architecture school, they moved out to the Pacific Northwest to start a new life together. While Tred officially launched in 2014, Megan and Lee have been designing custom homes together for well over a decade, starting with their own. Tred is uniquely positioned to help our clients because we have been in your shoes. We understand your concerns, hopes, and fears, and can guide you through our experience on both sides of the table.

Our Team

Megan Tremain, AIA

Principal + Owner

Megan's drive is helping others. After spending over a decade designing and managing large-scale commercial projects, she founded Tred with Lee to get back to her passion in architecture: developing a strong bond with her clients and helping them achieve their vision. She is the proud mother of two, a yogi, a newly minted cross-country skier, and still supports the Colts, despite the heartbreak.

Lee Edwards

Principal

Lee is just as comfortable with a hammer and hardhat as he is with a roll of trace paper. Lee's field-work expertise helps us communicate with our preferred builders more effectively. Prior to founding Tred with Megan, Lee ran a successful solo practice, Lee Edwards Residential Design. Lee considers himself a trophy husband and can still throw a mean fastball that his kids can't hit.

Jeff Matarrese, R.A.

Architect

Jeff's architectural background is in K-12 education and commercial projects. Besides being an architect he is constantly tinkering with our website, 3D modeling workflow, and project delivery systems. Jeff and his wife are the proud parents of a Golden Retriever named Onomatopoeia.
