About our firm

Our story

Tred Architecture + Design is a collaboration between husband-wife design team Megan Tremain and Lee Edwards. After meeting in architecture school, they moved out to the Pacific Northwest to start a new life together. Tred is uniquely positioned to help our clients because we have been in your shoes: our first project was our own home. We understand your concerns, hopes, and fears, and can guide you through our experience on both sides of the table.

We take a design-build mentality through an iterative design process. Builders and engineering consultants join the team while we are refining your design to ensure it will be well-executed and on budget.
