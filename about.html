About our firm

Our story

Tred Architecture + Design is the passion project of Megan Tremain and Lee Edwards since they were students together at Miami University in Ohio. After architecture school they moved out to the Pacific Northwest to start a life of their own together, designing and building what would be the first of many custom homes: their current home and the Tred office.

Tred is uniquely positioned to help our clients because we have been in your shoes. We have searched for the right property, spent months refining iteration after iteration, found financing, worked as an owner-contractor, and put in sweat equity to build our vision. We understand your worries, fears, and frustrations and can guide you through our experience on both sides of the table.

Let us help build your vision >>

We start by focusing on you. Learning about your goals, how you live, and your unique project conditions helps ensure that we move in the right direction from the beginning.

We take a design-build mentality through an iterative design process. Builders and engineering consultants join the team while we are refining your design to ensure it will be well-executed and on budget.

We advocate for you throughout construction. No job is truly complete until you turn the key. We are always available to you and your builder to answer questions on-site.
